The Little cloud Who Wanted to Paint

Once upon a time, high above the hills, there was a tiny cloud named pufi. While other clouds loved to float and bring rain, pufi had a dream-he wanted to paint the sky.

One day, pufi asked the sun, "Can I borrow your golden rays?" The sun smiled and gave him a handful of warm, shiny light.

Then pufi asked the rainbow, "Can I borrow your colors?" The rainbow laughed and shared red, blue, green and violet.

With a puff and a swirl, pufi painted the evening sky into the most beautiful sunset anyone had ever seen. The birds sang, the children clapped, and even the stars twinkled early to watch.

From that day on, pufi wasn't just a cloud-he was the little painter of the sky.
